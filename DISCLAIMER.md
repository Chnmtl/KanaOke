> 🌐 **Language / Dil:** **English** · _(Türkçe çevirisi istek üzerine eklenebilir.)_

This document explains what KanaOke is, how it relates to the third-party
services and content it touches, and the boundaries it operates within.

## What KanaOke is

KanaOke is a **personal, non-commercial portfolio and educational project**. Its
purpose is to demonstrate the developer's skills and to study Japanese through song lyrics.

It is **not a product, not a business, and is not monetized.** There are no
paid features, subscriptions, or advertisements. Voluntary donations, if ever
accepted, do not constitute a sale of any content or service.

## Scope of operation

KanaOke is intentionally kept within a small, low-impact footprint:

- **Limited audience.** There is no public, at-scale deployment.
- **No commercialization.** See above and the [LICENSE](LICENSE).
- **Personal study use.** Features exist to help an individual understand the
  text of a song they are already listening to through their own account.
